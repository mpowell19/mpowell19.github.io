The Project

As expected, temperatures tend to correlate depending on the relative distance from the equator. But is distance really the biggest factor?

For the purpose of this analysis, I wanted to study other factors such as cloud coverage, humidity, temperature, and wind speed to gain a better understanding of what impacts weather patterns and how those correlate with latitude and temperature.
